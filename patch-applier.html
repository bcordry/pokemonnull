How to Patch Pokémon Null

  1. Obtain a clean Pokémon Emerald ROM.
    We cannot provide ROMs. You must legally obtain your own copy of Pokémon Emerald(U)(TrashMan) in .gba format.
  2. Download the Pokémon Null patch file.
    You can get the latest patch from the Pokémon Null Discord server,
  3. Go to the online patcher.
    Click the Apply Pokémon Null Patch button above to open the online patcher tool (RomPatcher.js).
  4. Upload your ROM and the patch file.
    In the patcher, select your clean Emerald ROM as the ROM file and the Pokémon Null .bps patch as the Patch file.
  5. Apply the patch.
    Click Apply patch. When finished, download the new, patched ROM.
  6. Play Pokémon Null!
    Load the patched ROM in your favorite GBA emulator and enjoy the adventure.
Having trouble?
  • Make sure your ROM is a clean, unmodified Pokémon Emerald(U)(TrashMan).gba file. We cannot and will not provide that here and you may not ask for it in the Discord.
  • Do not use a randomizer or previously patched ROM.
  • If you get errors, try a different browser or clear your cache.
  • For more help, join our Discord community.
